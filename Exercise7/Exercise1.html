How a Realist Hero Rebuilt the Kingdom

by: Dojyomaru




One of my favorite book is a japanese light novel that is called How a Realist Hero Rebuilt the Kingdom. As the story progresses it's title also change to fit the current story in it. The book is a fantasy comedy for it's genre. This book has a total of 15 volume and still ongoing as of the moment. The story has a web novel version which is early draft for the published book.
